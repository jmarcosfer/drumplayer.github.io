Practice Buddy

Welcome to Practice Buddy, a site that brings together simple media and tools to get you on your daily practice as quickly as possible with as little hassle as necessary.

Media includes:

  • Images: short written music sheet snippets of patterns and grooves for you to practice
  • Audio: some written exercises will be accompanied by a recording showing you how it sounds when played
  • Videos of well-known drummers playing a given exercise/part

Tools include:

  1. An exercise/groove aggregator working under the hood to power the media displayed for the "Exercise of the Day" section. Media will be mainly gathered initially from DrummerWorld.
  2. A metronome app to help your time-keeping practice and play along with during your session
  3. an empty line to check github desktop is working
